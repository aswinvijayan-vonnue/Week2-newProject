History

1976
Founded on April 1, 1976 by Steve Jobs,and onald Wayne.
1977
Incorporated as Apple computer Inc and launched the apple II, which became one of the first highly successfull mass produced microcomputers.
1997
Facing bankruptcy,Apple acquired NeXT,bringing steve Jobs back to the company as ceo.
2023
Introduced the Apple Vision Pro,entering the spatial computing market.

Mission

Product Excellence: The belief that their purpose is to produce excellent products that positively impact the world.

Technological Ownership: A commitment to own and control the primary technologies behind the products they make.

Strategic Focus: The discipline to say no to thousands of projects in order to focus only on the few that make a significant,meaningful contribution
